# FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

### I am sick. Should I go home?

**Yes!** Make sure to tell me that you are going home. You do not need to ask permission unless we are scheduled to do something like a meeting.

### Can I work from home?

**Yes**, if your work can be done from home and you let me know and you have no other commitments.

### Can I take a vacation?

**Yes**, if you let me know very much in advance (like weeks in advance). Everyone needs a break at some point. **All students get 2 weeks of vacation per year excluding federal holidays.**

## Questions?

If you have questions about any of these policies or need clarification, please reach out on Slack or during your one-on-one meetings.
